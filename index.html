Business Name

Guided

Description of Services

Guided is a next-generation travel app that helps users plan and manage their trips with ease. The app provides an all-in-one platform to search and book hotels, flights, and tours — all powered by trusted partners like Aviasales, Hotellook, and Tiqets.

Unlike traditional travel apps, Guided includes a smart co-pilot assistant that delivers real-time weather alerts, trip tips, and intelligent nudges throughout the journey. You can edit, organize, and manage your full itinerary in one clean timeline.

Key features include:

  • Hotel, flight, and tour booking from one place
  • AI assistant for full trip planning and recommendations
  • Natural-language search like “flights to Paris next week”
  • Chronological trip cart and editable bookings
  • Smart filters by budget, rating, airline, and more
  • Real-time weather alerts and tips while you travel
