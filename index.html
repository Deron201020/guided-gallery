What We Offer

Guided is a next-generation travel assistant that helps users plan, book, and manage their trips with ease. The app consolidates hotel, flight, and tour bookings in one clean interface and enhances trips with real-time insights and a built-in AI assistant.

  • Hotel, flight, and tour booking in one place
  • Real-time weather alerts and travel nudges
  • AI assistant to build and edit full trip plans
  • Chronological cart to manage bookings
  • Smart filters by budget, rating, airline, and more
  • Natural-language search like “hotels in Rome next week”
