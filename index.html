FAQ

Why does Stripe need my business website?

Stripe uses your website to verify your business, ensure compliance, and understand what you offer. The site must always be live and accessible.

What information is required?

Your business name and a clear description of services, contact info, refund/cancellation/return policy, and any legal limitations must be visible.

Common reasons for rejection

  • Page is inaccessible or password-protected
  • Incomplete business details
  • Only a social media handle provided (not full URL)
  • Website under construction
  • Geo-blocking enabled
