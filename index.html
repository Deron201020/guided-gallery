What is Guided?

Guided is a travel app that brings together everything you need to plan a trip — from flights and hotels to tours and personalized itineraries — all in one simple interface.

Instead of flipping between websites and booking engines, you can search manually or ask our AI assistant to handle it. Just type something like "Flights to Tokyo" and you’ll get organized, relevant results instantly.

Why Guided?

Trip planning has become more chaotic than ever — open 10 tabs, compare prices, check the weather, read reviews… It’s a mess. Guided solves this by offering smart filters, live weather integration, and trustworthy data sources like Aviasales, Tiqets, and Hotellook, all in one place.

We built Guided to feel natural, intuitive, and even enjoyable. Whether you're planning a family vacation or a solo backpacking trip, Guided saves time and cuts stress.
